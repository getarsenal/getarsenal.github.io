ARSENAL — PRIVACY POLICY

What we collect
Arsenal stores your firearm and ammunition inventory data locally on your device. If you use the Squad social features, we collect your email address and display name via Supabase for authentication. Your public squad stats (readiness score, weapon count, round count, achievement count) are stored on Supabase servers to power the leaderboard.

What we never share
Your specific firearm details — serial numbers, calibers, purchase prices, photos, and service records — are stored only on your device and optional Google Drive. They are never transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party.

Google Drive sync
If you enable Google Drive sync, your encrypted inventory data is stored in your personal Google Drive account. Arsenal accesses only the single file it creates (arsenal_data.json). We do not access any other Google Drive content.

Squad / Social features
Squad features require account creation via Supabase. Your email is used only for authentication. Messages and activity feed posts are visible to other Squad members. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Squad → YOU tab.

Data retention
Local inventory data is retained until you clear it or uninstall the app. Squad account data is retained until you delete your account. Google Drive data is retained in your own Google account.

Children
Arsenal is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under 17 years of age.

Contact
For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us through the app's Squad feature or open a GitHub issue at getarsenal.app.

ONE-TIME SETUP — ~20 MINUTES
STEP 1 OF 4
CREATE GOOGLE CLOUD PROJECT
Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your Google account.

Click "Select a project" → "New Project" → name it ARSENAL → click Create.
STEP 2 OF 4
ENABLE GOOGLE DRIVE API
In your new project, go to APIs & Services → Library.

Search for "Google Drive API" → click it → click Enable.
STEP 3 OF 4
CREATE OAUTH CLIENT ID
Go to APIs & Services → Credentials.

Click "Create Credentials" → "OAuth Client ID".

If prompted to configure consent screen: choose External, fill in app name ARSENAL, your email, save.

Back in Credentials: Application type = Web application. Under Authorized JavaScript origins add:
https://getarsenal.app

Click Create. Copy the Client ID shown.
STEP 4 OF 4
PASTE YOUR CLIENT ID
Paste your Google OAuth Client ID below and tap Connect. You'll be asked to sign in with Google once, then sync is automatic.
